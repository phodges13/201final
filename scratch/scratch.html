My Favorite Show: Arrested Development

The Characters

George and Lucille Bluth

The parents of the Bluth Family. George was the CEO of the Bluth family company until he was arrested for building houses in the Middle East. His wife Lucille has her own way of leading the family from behind the scenes and trying to manipulate her children into doing what she wants.

Michael Bluth

Michael is a family man who's just trying to hold it together while his family starts to fall apart.

George Michael Bluth

Michael's teenage son who usually keeps to himself, but often finds himself in unique romantic situations.

Lindsay Bluth and Tobias FÃ¼nke

Lindsay is Michael's twin sister and wife to Tobias. Lindsay is a shopaholic who lives for fashion. Tobias is a certified psychiatrist and aspiring actor. Their marriage has many ups and downs, but at the end of the day, Lindsay and Tobias only care about each other... and their daughter.

Gob Bluth

Michael's older brother. Gob is a goofball magician who just want's his father's approval.

Buster Bluth

The youngest Bluth brother. Buster feels smothered by his mother but is also scared to spread his wings and become his own person.

The Best Seasons in Order and Favorite Episodes

  1. Season 1
    2. With some of the most iconic episodes and storylines, the first season is easily the best season of "Arrested Development".
    • "Pier Pressure" (Episode 10)
    • "Key Decisions" (Episode 4)
    • "Pilot" (Episode 1)
  2. Season 3
    3. This was such a silly season and the story came full circle for the Bluth family with heavy nods to the pilot in the season finale.
    • "Development Arrested" (Episode 13)
    • "The Cabin Show" (Episode 1)
    • "Fakin' It" (Episode 10)
  3. Season 2
    4. This is a good season with a lot of memorable moments. Most of the memes related to the show come out of season 2.
    • "The One Where Michael Leaves" (Epsiode 1)
    • "Amigos!" (Episode 3)
    • "Good Grief" (Episode 4)
  4. Seasons 4 and 5
    5. Once Netflix got a hold of the franchise, everything started to go downhill. The writers tried to pick up the story where season 3 left off, but the actors looked noticably older and the story felt drawn out. I'm just going to leave these two seasons at the bottom together because they're honestly so bad.
